Trackgenix

Time is in your hands. We are here to help you manage it and make it perform at its best.

Trackgenix sistem is designed to facilitate the recording of hours worked by each employee in each project and work team. It can be used by multiple roles in the company and will help with the managment of multiple proyects at the same time.

Funcionalities

Timesheet

The most important funcionality of the sistem is the timesheet. Perfect for time track of the employees and diferent proyects. Helps you for a faster and accurate managment.

Reports

Every employee can make reports for the diferents proyects. Then you could check them in the form of graphics and text.

Resource management

Perfect for reduce costs by helping you manage salaries and expenses of the diferent proyects.

Roles

The system is designed for any role in the company with its different functionalities and work areas.
